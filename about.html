Our Story

Shiv Shakti Services, under the umbrella of Karwal Group, was founded with a simple yet powerful vision: to make quality financial services accessible to everyone. What started as a small financial consultancy has grown into a comprehensive financial services provider, serving hundreds of satisfied clients across various financial needs.

Our journey began with a commitment to understanding each client's unique financial situation and providing personalized solutions that truly make a difference. Over the years, we have built a reputation for reliability, expertise, and genuine care for our clients' financial well-being.

Today, we offer a wide range of services including loans, insurance, investment planning, and financial advisory services. Our team of experienced professionals continues to uphold the values that founded our company: integrity, excellence, and unwavering commitment to our clients' success.
